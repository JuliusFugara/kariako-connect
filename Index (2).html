02 — Problem & Solution
Why KariakooConnect Exists

Kariakoo is East Africa's largest wholesale market — yet trade operates on phone calls, spreadsheets, and mistrust.

🔴

The Problem Today

  • Buyers in Zambia or Zimbabwe have no trusted way to source from Kariakoo without traveling physically
  • No verified product listings — quality is unknown until goods arrive
  • Payment fraud is rampant; sellers demand 100% cash upfront, buyers refuse
  • Zero real-time delivery visibility — shipments vanish for days
  • Cross-border FX is done manually at black-market rates with massive losses
  • No formal quotation system — WhatsApp negotiations with no paper trail
  • Small retailers cannot access wholesale MOQ (minimum order quantities) alone
  • Savings are kept under mattresses — no business banking for informal traders
🟢

The KariakooConnect Solution

  • Verified digital storefront for every Kariakoo seller with photo catalogues & pricing tiers
  • In-app RFQ (Request For Quotation) system with PDF export and digital acceptance
  • Escrow wallet holds 45% upfront safely until buyer confirms delivery
  • Live GPS route tracker across TZ → ZW / ZM / MW borders with milestone alerts
  • Multi-currency wallets with M-Pesa, EcoCash, Airtel Money, PayPal & Visa integration
  • Group-buy feature for retailers to collectively meet wholesale MOQ thresholds
  • In-app savings accounts with interest for both buyers and sellers
  • KYC-verified accounts to build trust scores for repeat business

04 — Core Features
5 Features That Define the App

Every feature solves a specific pain point that keeps Kariakoo trade informal and risky.

01
🏪
Verified Seller Storefronts

Sellers create KYC-verified business profiles and list products with high-resolution photos, wholesale MOQ pricing, retail pricing, stock availability, and delivery lead times. Buyers can browse by category (Consumer Goods, Clothes, Accessories), apply filters, and save favourites. Every seller earns a Trust Score based on transaction history, response time, and dispute resolution.

KYC Verification Product Catalogue Trust Score Stock Sync
02
📋
In-App RFQ & Quotation Engine

Buyers submit a Request For Quotation directly to sellers specifying product, quantity, delivery destination and preferred currency. Sellers respond with a formal quote including unit price, total, shipping cost and estimated delivery date. Quotes are accepted digitally and auto-generate an order contract. Full negotiation thread is preserved as an audit trail. PDF export for offline records.

RFQ System Quote Negotiation PDF Export Order Contract
03
🛡️
Escrow Wallet & 45/55 Payment Model

When an order is confirmed, the buyer pays 45% upfront into a KariakooConnect escrow wallet — not directly to the seller. This protects both parties. The seller receives the 45% only after confirming dispatch. The remaining 55% is paid by the buyer upon confirmed delivery. If a dispute arises, escrow funds are frozen pending platform mediation. Sellers can also access a business savings account within the wallet with daily interest.

Escrow Protection 45% Upfront Savings Account Dispute Mediation
04
🗺️
Live Cross-Border Delivery Tracker

Interactive maps for Tanzania, Zimbabwe, Zambia and Malawi. Shipments are tracked in real-time via GPS pings from logistics partner drivers. Buyers see their order's current location on the map with estimated arrival. Automated milestone notifications at key checkpoints: Dispatched → Border Crossing → In-Country Transit → Out for Delivery → Delivered. Supports multi-leg shipments (e.g. Dar → Lusaka → Lilongwe).

GPS Tracking 4-Country Maps Milestone Alerts Multi-leg Routes
05
💳
Multi-Modal Payment & Remittance Hub

Seamlessly pay in your local method — M-Pesa (Tanzania), EcoCash (Zimbabwe), Airtel Money (Zambia/Malawi), PayPal (international), Visa/Mastercard (global), or in-app wallet. Built-in currency conversion at competitive rates for cross-border transactions. Remittance feature lets diaspora buyers fund orders from abroad. Sellers receive payouts in their preferred currency with instant settlement or scheduled batch payout.

M-Pesa EcoCash Airtel Money PayPal Visa/MC Remittance
+
🤝
Group Buy for Retailers

Small retailers who can't meet a seller's wholesale MOQ alone can join or create a Group Buy. The platform pools orders from multiple buyers for the same product, reaching MOQ together and splitting the shipment on delivery. Automated contribution tracking, shared escrow, and pro-rated delivery costs. This democratises wholesale pricing for every small business across the region.

Pool Orders MOQ Splitting Shared Escrow

09 — Roadmap
Build Plan & Launch Timeline

From MVP to multi-country expansion in 18 months.

Phase 1 — Months 1–4
MVP Launch: Tanzania Only
  • Seller onboarding with KYC and product listing (max 20 beta sellers from Kariakoo)
  • Buyer account creation and RFQ / quotation flow
  • M-Pesa and Visa payment integration with escrow wallet
  • Basic Tanzania map with manual shipment status updates
  • Android app (Kotlin) + responsive web platform
  • In-app messaging between buyer and seller
Phase 2 — Months 5–9
Live GPS Tracking + Zimbabwe & Zambia
  • Driver GPS tracker app (Flutter) integrated with buyer-facing maps
  • Zimbabwe (EcoCash) and Zambia (Airtel Money) payment methods added
  • Cross-border route maps: TZ → ZW and TZ → ZM corridors
  • Savings account feature within the seller wallet
  • Group Buy feature for retail buyers
  • Trust Score algorithm launched with seller reviews
Phase 3 — Months 10–14
Malawi Expansion + Financial Products
  • Malawi coverage with Airtel Malawi money integration
  • PayPal and Remittance hub for diaspora buyers
  • Business loans pilot for verified high-volume sellers (BNPL model)
  • Analytics dashboard for sellers (sales trends, top buyers, inventory alerts)
  • Automated customs documentation generation for cross-border shipments
  • iOS app release
Phase 4 — Months 15–18
Scale, AI & Regional Dominance
  • AI-powered product matching: recommend buyers to sellers based on purchase history
  • Expand to Kenya (Nairobi), Mozambique, and DRC
  • White-label logistics: KariakooConnect branded delivery service
  • Open API for third-party ERP and inventory systems
  • Series A fundraise targeting $5M for regional expansion
