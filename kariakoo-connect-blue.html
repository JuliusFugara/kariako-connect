02 — Problem & Solution
Why KariakooConnect Exists

Kariakoo is East Africa's largest wholesale market — yet trade operates on phone calls, spreadsheets, and mistrust.

🔴

The Problem Today

  • Buyers in Zambia or Zimbabwe have no trusted way to source from Kariakoo without traveling physically
  • No verified product listings — quality is unknown until goods arrive
  • Payment fraud is rampant; sellers demand 100% cash upfront, buyers refuse
  • Zero real-time delivery visibility — shipments vanish for days
  • Cross-border FX is done manually at black-market rates with massive losses
  • No formal quotation system — WhatsApp negotiations with no paper trail
  • Small retailers cannot access wholesale MOQ alone
  • Savings are kept under mattresses — no business banking for informal traders
🟢

The KariakooConnect Solution

  • Verified digital storefronts for every Kariakoo seller with photo catalogues & pricing tiers
  • In-app RFQ (Request For Quotation) system with PDF export and digital acceptance
  • Escrow wallet holds 45% upfront safely until buyer confirms delivery
  • Live GPS route tracker across TZ → ZW / ZM / MW borders with milestone alerts
  • Multi-currency wallets with M-Pesa, EcoCash, Airtel Money, PayPal & Visa
  • Group-buy feature for retailers to collectively meet wholesale MOQ thresholds
  • In-app savings accounts with interest for both buyers and sellers
  • KYC-verified accounts to build trust scores for repeat business

04 — Core Features
5 Features That Define the App

Every feature solves a specific pain point that keeps Kariakoo trade informal and risky.

01
🏪
Verified Seller Storefronts

Sellers create KYC-verified business profiles and list products with high-resolution photos, wholesale MOQ pricing, retail pricing, stock availability, and delivery lead times. Every seller earns a Trust Score based on transaction history and dispute resolution.

KYC Verification Product Catalogue Trust Score Stock Sync
02
📋
In-App RFQ & Quotation Engine

Buyers submit a Request For Quotation directly to sellers specifying product, quantity, delivery destination and preferred currency. Sellers respond with a formal quote. Quotes are accepted digitally and auto-generate an order contract with PDF export.

RFQ System Quote Negotiation PDF Export Order Contract
03
🛡️
Escrow Wallet & 45/55 Payment Model

When an order is confirmed, the buyer pays 45% into KariakooConnect escrow — not directly to the seller. The seller receives 45% on dispatch, and the remaining 55% is released upon confirmed delivery. Dispute mediation freezes funds pending resolution.

Escrow Protection 45% Upfront Savings Account Dispute Mediation
04
🗺️
Live Cross-Border Delivery Tracker

Interactive maps for Tanzania, Zimbabwe, Zambia and Malawi. Shipments tracked via GPS pings from logistics partner drivers. Milestone notifications at Dispatched → Border Crossing → In-Country Transit → Out for Delivery → Delivered. Supports multi-leg routes.

GPS Tracking 4-Country Maps Milestone Alerts Multi-leg Routes
05
💱
Multi-Currency Wallet & Payments

Buyers and sellers hold wallets in TZS, ZWL, ZMW, and MWK with live FX rates. Seamless integration with M-Pesa, EcoCash, Airtel Money, PayPal, and Visa/Mastercard. Remittance hub for diaspora buyers funding orders from abroad.

4 Currencies Live FX Rates 6+ Methods Instant Payouts
